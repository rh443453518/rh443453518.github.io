Welcome to my portfolio page.

I am an enthusiastic researcher interested in the interplay of Social Science and Data Science, especially in applying and creating methods inspired by data to enhance aspects of urban and social development. I am a second-year in the MS Program of Regional Science at Cornell University. Currently, my major field of study is Urban and Regional Economy while my minor field of study is Information System.

My research of interests include Information Economics, Social Data Mining, Urban Computing, Data Visualization, and Machine Learning for Public Policy.
